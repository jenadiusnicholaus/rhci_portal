About RHCI

Building a bridge between generous donors and patients in need of surgical care in Tanzania

Our Vision

A Tanzania where every underserved patient, regardless of their economic background, has equal access to quality healthcare through an integrated, transparent, and scalable digital health system.

  • Universal healthcare access
  • Digital health integration
  • Economic equality in healthcare

Our Mission

To expand access to essential, life-saving surgeries for underserved patients especially children in Tanzania by harnessing digital technology, strengthening community partnerships, and working closely with government structures.

  • Digital technology integration through CareLink
  • Community partnership building
  • Government collaboration
  • Free and timely treatment
Our Story

The Legacy of REIZA

REIZA Health Care Initiative (RHCI) was born from the lives and legacies of three children who changed everything: Rehema, Isaac, and Zamda. Their names form REIZA, and their courage is the foundation of our mission.

We met these children in desperate need of care, bright and innocent lives held back not by illness alone, but by poverty. Despite the urgency, Zamda passed away before receiving help. Rehema and Isaac also developed several health complications. Their passing left a permanent mark on our hearts and a fierce determination in our souls.

Never again should a child die because healthcare was out of reach. From this pain grew purpose. RHCI now works tirelessly across Tanzania to identify, support, and treat underserved patients.

Through CareLink, our mobile app, we connect patients and health professionals in real-time, allowing those who can pay to access care while their payments help fund those who cannot. This creates a sustainable cycle of care, compassion, and equity.

CareLink Platform

Technology for Healthcare Equity

CareLink is RHCI's innovative mobile app that revolutionizes healthcare accessibility in Tanzania. It connects patients with healthcare professionals in real-time, facilitating both paid and subsidized care delivery.

  • Real-time patient-doctor connections
  • Transparent fundraising platform
  • Cross-subsidized healthcare model
  • Digital patient identification
